Below are some of my favorite fantasy novel series that I've read to date! This list is not all inclusive but don't worry! There will be some honorable mentions at the end of our journey!

Lady of Darkness
by Melissa Roehrich

This fanstasy novel series comes in 5 pieces, each one better than the last! Princess by day and assassin by night. What more could a girl want? Well, actually, a girl could want a lot of things.. Magical powers, the ability to summon a shadow dragon... the you ride into battle against your enemies... with your obscenely hot man covering your back and making love to you at every opportunity. Not to mention the family you find along the way.

Blood & Steel
by Helen Scheureur

Want a man that is obsessed with you? Look no further! Long, dark hair and a full beard to boot? Again, LOOK NO FURTHER. Come fight the shadow creeping in on the midrealms of Thezmarr in this plot-twist ridden series. Hope you came ready to eat Biscuits and Pancakes!

Mistborn
by Brandon Sanderson

Best magic system you will ever read from the master himself. Brandon will be remembered in hundreds of years for his contributions to fantasy! All hail the King of Magic!

Lord of the Rings
by J.R.R. Tolkien

Okay, I know I called Brandon Sanderson the King of Magic, but Tolkien is the King of Worldbuilding. I mean, come on! I will rave and rave about these books, till the end of my days.

Fourth Wing
by Rebecca Yarros

CAUTION! Dragons and banter ahead! If you like survivng against all odds and epic cliff hangers, this one's for you! Apply to Basgiath War College today!

Acceptance rate: 100, Graduation rate: 1%.
